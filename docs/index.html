Walrus Association

We come in peace, ready for war. We are an association of individuals united by the pursuit of both the physical and the cerebral. This idea manifests in us through our art, our dedication to strength, and our hunger for knowledge. But what is this war? It is the relentless, self-imposed battle to always be prepared. Our associates are racers, painters, physicists, soldiers, mathematicians, and more. We hold ourselves to the highest standards of integrity. Associates are steadfast, honorable, generous in spirit, open-handed in camaraderie, and unwavering in resolve. We meet challenge with courage, face failure with dignity, and win with grace.
